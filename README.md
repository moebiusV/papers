## [12 Hz AC Power Grid for national sovereignty](papers/12hz-power-grid/)

Low-frequency AC grid architecture using commodity steel for large power transformers.
A solution to the global transformer shortage — caused by limited supply of GOES
(grain-oriented electrical steel) and the complexity of modern large transformers —
that is modular, locally manufacturable, and easier to install and maintain in every dimension.

## [Entfesselt](papers/entfesselt/)

*Vom Wagner zum Walther: Siegfried Entfesselt* — liberating Old Norse from Italian grammar.
The differences between English, German, Dutch, and Scandinavian are greatly exaggerated
by 18th-century prescriptive standardization campaigns modeled on Latin. When the prescriptive
layer is removed, the true family resemblance becomes visible. And none of the languages have
diverged as far from their Old Norse, Old English, and Gothic origins as we've been led to believe.
